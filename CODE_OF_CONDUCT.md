# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Accepting feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Trolling or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code.
They may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

To report abusive behavior, open a private security advisory in GitHub or
contact the maintainers directly.
